CS 313 | Web Engineering II

08 Teach : Team Activity

Special Instructions for Online Students

You need to coordinate a one hour synchronous meeting with your group for this activity.

Overview

For this week's team readiness activity, please virtually meet at your regular time.

Then, use this meeting to help one another with the weekly assignment to set up your environments.

Instructions

Core Requirements

  1. Install and configure NetBeans.

  2. Create a Java Web application, and add a simple servlet that renders the text, "Hello Java World".

  3. Create a new Java Web application at Heroku, deploy your application to it, and ensure that you can see the Hello World Servlet there.

Stretch Challenges

The expectation for this week's stretch challenge is to ensure that everyone else on your team can complete the core requirements.

  1. At least one other person has completed the core requirements, and can deploy and view their servlet at Heroku.

  2. More than one other person has completed the core requirements, and can deploy and view their servlet at Heroku.

  3. All members of the team (including those not present for the team activity) have completed the core requirements, and can deploy and view their servlet at Heroku.

Instructor's Solution

Due to the nature of this assignment, there is no instructor's solution, other than the provided preparation material. When you report your results on the quiz, you may indicate that you looked at the instructor's solution and put "not applicable" or something similar in the question that asks for the insights you gained from it.

Submission

When you have finished this activity, please fill out the assessment in I-Learn. You are welcome to complete any additional parts of this activity by yourself or with others after your meeting before submitting the assessment.
