CS 313 | Web Engineering II

14 Teach : Peer Review

Overview

As a part of your final week, each of you will evaluate the group projects of your classmates. This rating has a few purposes:

  1. Let you see the awesome things your classmates have built.

  2. Practice critiquing others' work in a constructive manner.

  3. Obtain good feedback from others.

  4. Determine our class app winners. This style of presentation/feedback is being done in lieu of formal class oral presentations and questions, so I hope you will take them seriously in both your own presentation and in your evaluation of others.

Also, your rating will factor into the group's grade.

Instructions

After Monday morning at 8:00 AM, go through each project in the class app store wiki page. You will want to spend some time on their app store page, but then also look at and play around with their web application.

For each site, leave comments in the discussion forum. If you are the first person to review an app, please create a new thread for it. Otherwise, post to the existing thread for that project. You should try to point out one thing that is working well, and one suggestion for improvement.

After going through each application, please rate the group projects on a scale of 1-5 stars in the separate I-Learn item.

You should rate your own project (and only your own) as a 6.
