Course Schedule

This course is organized at the week level.

Weekly Rhythm

Each week, you'll be expected to do the following:

  1. Due Tuesday (possibly earlier) - Prepare Assignment / Quiz.

    Each week you need to complete the prepare assignment and quiz by the start of your team meeting or by Tuesday night, whichever comes earliest. For face-to-face, this means before your first class period of the week. For online students, this needs to be completed before your pre-arranged meeting time.

  2. Due Wednesday - Team Activity

    Each week you will work with your team to complete an activity. Face-to-Face students will complete the assignment in class, during the first class period of the week. Online students are responsible to arrange a synchronous meeting with their team prior to Wednesday night at 11:00PM.

  3. Due Saturday - Assignment / Project Deliverable

    You will have a "prove" assignment each week consisting of a programming assignment, or a portion of your programming project. This must be submitted by Saturday at 11:00PM.
