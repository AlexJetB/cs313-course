Course Schedule

This course is organized at the week level.

Weekly Rhythm

Each week, you'll be expected to do the following:

  • Complete the Prepare assignment and quiz before class on Wednesday.

  • Face-to-Face students will complete the Teach assignment and quiz in class on Wednesday. Online students will complete the Teach assignment and quiz on their own.

  • Complete the Prove assignment and quiz by Saturday at midnight.

  • Complete the weekly self-assessment by Saturday at midnight.
