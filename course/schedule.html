CS 313 | Web Engineering II

Course Schedule

This course is organized at the week level.

Weekly Rhythm

Each week, you'll be expected to do the following:

  • Due Tuesday (ish) - Complete the Prepare assignment and quiz before your team meeting. For face-to-face, this means before your first class period of the week. For online students, this needs to be completed before your pre-arranged meeting time.

  • Due Wednesday - Complete a weekly team activity. Face-to-Face students will complete the assignment and quiz in class, the first period of the week. Online students are responsible to arrange a synchronous meeting with their team prior to Wednesday night at 11:00PM.

  • Due Saturday - Complete the Prove assignment by Saturday at 11:00PM.
