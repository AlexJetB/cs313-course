Setting up MySQL at OpenShift

  1. Sign in to your account at OpenShift.com

  2. Go to the applications tab and select your application.

  3. Click "Add MySQL 5.5", and agree to "Add the Cartiridge".

  4. Make note of your root username and password.

  5. On the applications page, under "Tools and Support", Click to Add "phpMyAdmin 4.0".

  6. You can now access phpMyAdmin, at a URL similar to: URL: https://php-sburton.rhcloud.com/phpmyadmin/
