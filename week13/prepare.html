Preparation Material

The preparation material this week is different than in weeks past.

For this week, your assignment is to learn about a new web technology that we have not discussed in class (and that you have not used previously), and come prepared to discuss it.

Specifically, be prepared to talk about points such as the following:

  • What is the main purpose of this technology (what was it designed for)?

  • What are some of its strengths and weaknesses compared to others that are designed for a similar purpose?

  • What are some of its limitations and areas where it should not be applied?

  • How does it compare to or possibly integrate with some of the technologies we have discussed in this course?

You need not prepare a formal presentation, and you shouldn’t spend hours upon hours on this research, but it should be more than a quick 20 minute skim.
