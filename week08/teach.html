CS 313 | Web Engineering II

08 Teach : Team Activity

Special Instructions for Online Students

You need to coordinate a one hour synchronous meeting with your group for this activity.

Overview

For this week's team activity, you will work together on a workshop from nodeschool.io .

Instructions

Ensure that you have node.js and npm installed, then follow the instructions from nodeschool.io to install and run the "learnyounode" workshopper.

Core Requirements

  1. Complete the "Hello World" and "Baby Steps" exercises.

  2. Complete the "My first I/O" and "My first async I/O" exercises.

  3. Complete the "Filtered ls" and "Make it modular" exercises.

Stretch Challenges

  1. Complete the "HTTP Client" and "HTTP Collect" exercises.

  2. Complete the "Juggling Async" and "Time Server" exercises.

  3. Complete the last three exercises.

Instructor's Solution

Due to the nature of this assignment, the instructor solution will be provided by the learnyounode module after each exercise.

Submission

When you have finished this activity, please fill out the assessment in I-Learn. You are welcome to complete any additional parts of this activity by yourself or with others after your meeting before submitting the assessment.
