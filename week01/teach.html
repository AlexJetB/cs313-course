01 Teach : Ice Breaker

Overview

The term "icebreakers" refers to ships designed to break up ice to facilitate travel through ice-packed regions. A flow of ice might well describe the atmosphere of an online class before it gets off the ground. Icebreakers are meant to put you at ease, get your mental juices flowing, and allow you to meet your fellow classmates. Have fun and be creative with the following exercise.

Two Truths And A Lie

Instructions

Your initial post should be made on Tuesday by 11:00 PM. Click on New Thread (above) to open a discussion box.

  • Tell us your name and where you are from.
  • List three interesting things about yourself. Two must be true and one must be a lie.

Example:

  • I own two iguanas./li>
  • I once shook hands with Bill Gates.
  • I love to water-ski.

Comment on at least five other posts indicating which of their statements you believe to be a lie. Feel free to comment on as many other posts as you would like, sharing connections you may have. These five posts should be done by Friday.

On Saturday, reveal which item on your list was a lie and add any details you would like others to know. This discussion board will remain open throughout the semester. Feel free to ask questions and continue getting to know your classmates.
